### Software Used:
GIMP

### Techniques Used:
Gaussian Blur, Lens Blur

### Font Used:
[Higure Gothic Black](https://fontfree.me/3410)

# Explanation

Separated into layers and applied Gaussian blur to each.

Then duplicated the background-only layer and added dots on top of it.

Applied lens blur to the dots.

Then placed the text.
